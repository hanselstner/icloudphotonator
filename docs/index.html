Installation

iCloudPhotonator is a free, open-source macOS app. Since it's not from the App Store, macOS will ask you to confirm on first launch.

1. Download

Download the DMG file and open it.

2. Install

Drag iCloudPhotonator to your Applications folder.

3. Launch

Double-click to launch. Grant Automation permission when prompted.

4. Ready

Grant Automation permission and start importing!

Your memories deserve better

Most of us started taking digital photos in the early 2000s. Since then, tens of thousands of images have piled up — on NAS boxes, external hard drives, old laptops, forgotten folders on your Mac. These aren't just files. They're your children's first steps, family celebrations, the faces of people who are no longer with us. But they sit on aging hardware that could fail any day. An old hard drive in a drawer isn't a backup — it's a ticking countdown. Apple Photos changes everything: face recognition, location search, curated memories, available on every device. But getting 50,000 photos in there? That's where it gets painful.

Scattered across devices

Photos on NAS, external drives, old laptops — hard to find, easy to lose.

Hardware doesn't last

Hard drives fail. NAS boxes age. One day those memories are just… gone.

Impossible to search

No face recognition, no location search, no curated memories. Your best photos are buried in a folder hierarchy nobody remembers.

Why Apple Photos?

Once your photos are in Apple Photos, they come alive. They're not just stored — they're searchable, organized, and available everywhere.

People Recognition

Find every photo of your daughter, your parents, your best friend — automatically, across decades of photos.

Places & Dates

Browse your photos by location and date. Relive that trip to Italy or your kid's birthday — without remembering which folder it's in.

Available Everywhere

With iCloud, your entire library syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Pull up any photo, anytime, from any device.

Import once. Keep forever.

Whether your photos live on a Synology NAS, a USB drive, or scattered across folders on your Mac — iCloudPhotonator imports them all into Apple Photos safely, incrementally, and without losing a single file.

Bulk Import

Import 10,000 to 100,000+ files in a single run.

Pause & Resume

Stop and continue anytime — never lose progress.

Smart Deduplication

No duplicate imports, ever. Hash-based detection keeps your library clean.

Auto-Recovery

Automatic escalation when Photos.app becomes unresponsive.

Multilingual

Configurable

Tune batch sizes, cooldowns, and restart intervals to your hardware.
